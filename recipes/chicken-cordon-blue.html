Good New Orleans Creole Gumbo

Description

A cordon bleu is a dish that traditionally consists of meat wrapped around cheese, then breaded and pan-fried or deep-fried. Many modern versions of the classic French recipe, such as this one, are baked.

Classic chicken cordon bleu is made with the following ingredients: chicken cutlets, cooked ham, Swiss cheese, bread crumbs, and simple spices. It's usually topped with a rich cream sauce spiked with a hint of Dijon mustard.

Ingredients

    nonstick cooking spray 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves ¼ teaspoon salt ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper 6 slices Swiss cheese 4 slices cooked ham ½ cup seasoned bread crumbs
  • nonstick cooking spray
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 6 slices Swiss cheese
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 slices cooked ham
  • ½ cup seasoned bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Step 1:Gather all ingredients. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Coat a 7x11-inch baking dish with nonstick cooking spray.
  2. Step 2: Pound chicken breasts to 1/4 inch thickness.
  3. Step 3: Sprinkle each piece of chicken on both sides with salt and pepper. Place 1 Swiss cheese slice and 1 ham slice on top of each breast.
  4. Step 4: Roll up each breast and secure with toothpicks.
  5. Step 5: Place in the prepared baking dish and sprinkle chicken evenly with bread crumbs.
  6. Step 6: Bake in the preheated oven until chicken is no longer pink, 30 to 35 minutes.
  7. Step 7: Remove from the oven, and place 1/2 Swiss cheese slice on top of each breast.
  8. Step 8: Return to the oven until cheese has melted, 3 to 5 minutes. Remove toothpicks and serve immediately.
